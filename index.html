ABOUT

Welcome to Pizzaland, home of 40 distinct kinds of pizza! Some you'll know quite well, some are regional specialties (shoutouts to New Haven, St. Louis and, of course, Chicago) and some are one-off examples that actually exist. You'd be hard-pressed to find the deep-fried Scottish pizza crunch outside of its native land, and jumbo slices are definitely a Washington, D.C.–area creation. Some aren't even pizza at all — sushi, Mexican and smoked salmon are more riffs on an idea than legitimate varietals, and whether calzone and stromboli belong to the pizza category is up for debate (but we wanted to illustrate 'em, so what the hell).
